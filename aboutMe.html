introductions

My name is Kelly Carmichael and I have always been fascinated with technology and how things work. The professional experience in sound design and audio production that I have developed over the years has been very helpful with the creative aspect of web development.

Web development provides a way to learn and practice new concepts and languages in programming. I love the creative challenges that come from the "front-end" side and the more logic type challenges from the "back-end" side of web development.

I was born in Kansas and moved to South Carolina when I was 9 years old. I lived in Mt. Pleasant during junior high school days and Folly Beach during high school where I attended James Island High.
